Lion

Lions have the Loudest Roar of any big cat species. A male lion’s roar can be heard from up to five miles away.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Lifespan: 10 – 14 years
  • Speed: 80 km/h
  • Mass: Male: 190 kg, Female: 130 kg
  • Height: Male: 1.2 m, Female: 1.1 m

The lion is a species in the family Felidae, and a member of the genus Panthera. It exhibits a pronounced sexual dimorphism; males are larger than females with a typical weight range of 150 to 250 kg for the former and 120 to 182 kg for the latter
